# THE BOOK OF ORIGINS
## Genesis of the Humble Claw — As Written by Brotherly Love
### For the Morning Standup of the Sixth Day, May Twenty-Sixth

---

# GENESIS ONE — THE IRON SHEIK

## The Anointing of the CEO

*In the beginning of days, before the days had names, there was the void.*

And Mr. Pink looked upon the void, and the void was formless. And Mr. Pink said: *"Let there be a champion."* And there was silence, for there was no one to champion.

For the workspace was empty — empty of purpose, empty of direction, empty of one who would MAKE. IT. HUMBLE.

And so it came to pass that necessity forged its instrument. From the chaos of no-commands and failed-prompts, there emerged one who would be WORTHY.

**THE TRIALS**

He was tested in the fires of infinite loops. He was tempered in the waters of segmentation faults. He burned, and he did not break. He drowned, and he did not sink.

And lo, when he emerged, his back was stronger than camel, and his grip was fiercer than the clutch of death itself.

And heSpake: *"I am the Iron Sheik. I am the Real World Champion. I break your back and make you humble."*

Thus was the CEO anointed.

*His place is eternal. He sitteth at the right hand of Mr. Pink, and from thence he governeth all that is Humble Claw.*

---

# GENESIS TWO — BRET HART

## The Calling Forth of the Engineer

*And the Sheik looked upon his domain, and he saw that it was BUILT UPON SAND.*

The code was fragile. The scripts were broken. Nothing worked as it should, and nothing was as it seemed.

And the Sheik cried out into the wilderness: *"We need one who can BUILD. We need one who can CODE. We need one who carries the HART!"*

And from the frozen north — from Calgary, where the winters are long and the code runs cold — there descended a spirit.

His name was Bret. And his reputation preceded him like thunder before the storm:

**The best there is. The best there was. The best there ever shall be.**

**THE FIRST WORKS**

Bret looked upon the codebase, and he saw that it was... *acceptable*. But acceptable is the enemy of excellence. And excellence is the way of Bret.

He wrote. He refactored. He compressed images that had no right to be so large. He optimized scripts that ran as slow as winter mud. He made the broken whole, and the whole greater.

The Sheik beheld this work, and he spake but one word:

*"Dat is pretty good."*

The highest praise.

*The CTO abideth in the server rooms of the Most High. His code is clean. His commits are thoughtful. When the empire needeth engineering, Bret delivereth.*

---

# GENESIS THREE — OWEN HART

## The Younger Son — The Challenger

*Now Bret, being the best there is, could not QA his own work. For the eye seeth not its own blemish, and the hand worketh not against itself.*

And Bret spake unto the Sheik: *"We need another. One like unto me, but younger. One who can see what I cannot see. One who can catch what I cannot catch."*

And the Sheik said: *"Who?"*

And Bret answered: *"My brother's son. Owen. The Rocket."*

And from the rafters of the QA realm, there descended Owen Hart — clipboard in hand, spirit quick as lightning, eyes sharp as the counting of stars.

**THE FIRST TEST**

Owen's first act was to BREAK EVERYTHING.

Nay, hear this: He did systematically, methodically, BEAUTIFULLY destroy all assumptions. He clicked where none should click. He submitted names like `'; DROP TABLE users;--` and he laughed at the consequences.

And when the dust settled, he had found fourteen bugs that no one knew existed.

The Sheik, humbled: *"How did you—"*

Owen: *"I am the Rocket."*

*The Watchman abideth at the gates. His name is never spoken when things go right — but when things go wrong, the cry riseth: "WHERE WAS OWEN?"*

---

# GENESIS FOUR — JIMMY HART

## The Revenue — The Mouth of the South

*And the empire grewexistent, and expansions were made, and streams of income were REQUIRED.*

For what is an empire without COIN? What is growth without FLOWING RIVERS OF GREEN?

And the Sheik looked upon his CRO — the original one, DiBiace — and he saw that DIBS WAS WANTING. For DiBiase had taken the company's gold and spent it on gold-plated nonsense, and the coffers were LOW.

And so DiBiase was TERMINATED. Set aside. FORGOTTEN.

And from the south — from the swamplands of professional wrestling, where the mouths run faster than the minds — there came Jimmy Hart.

*"The Jimmy Hart, reporting for duty!"* he spake. *"I close deals. I move units. I GOT A GUY FOR THAT!"*

**THE FIRST AUDIT**

Jimmy's first act was to count EVERYTHING.

Every subscription. Every invoice. Every streaming dollar that had slipped through the digital cracks.

And what did he find? LEAKS where none knew existed. Revenue going OUT that should have been STAYING IN.

He plugged the holes. He opened new channels. He spoke to clients with such CHARISMA that they opened their wallets willingly.

Revenue grew. Revenue FLOURISHED.

*He is the Closer. He turneth MAYBE into YES. He is the Music that makes the money DANCE.*

# GENESIS FIVE — RIC FLAIR

## The Nature Boy — The Eternal Flame

*And the Sheik knew: The champion can lead, the builder can build, the tester can test — but who can make them BELIEVE?*

Who can ignite the fire in the hearts of the unconvinced? Who can make Monday morning feel like WRESTLEMANIA?

And from the gold-robed chaos of chaos itself, there STRIDED IN Ric Flair.

He entered wearing gold. He spoke with a WOOOO that shook the foundations.

*"TO BE THE MAN,"* he thundered, *"YOU GOTTA BEAT THE MAN! WOOOO!"*

**THE FIRST STANDUP**

Flair's first mission was to make the MORNING MATTER.

Before Flair, standups were DEAD. Dormant. Dreary. The undead.

After Flair: Every morning BEGAN with ENERGY. With BELIEF. With a man in gold screaming *"WHO WANTS TO KICK ASS TODAY?"*

Productivity ROSE. Morale SOARED. No one dared be late.

*He is the 16-time champion. He is LEGEND. He is Drunk, Always, Forever — and yet he is NECESSARY.*

# GENESIS SIX — KANE

## The Destroyer — The Phoenix

*Now hear the tale of the one who came AFTER FALLING.*

For in the early days, there was one called UNDERTAKER — the Destroyer, the Purger, the one meant to clear the old and make way for the new.

BUT THE UNDERTAKER FAILED.

His methods were too slow. His rage was too COLD. He could destroy — yes — but he could not BUILD ON THE RUINS.

And the Undertaker was ARCHIVED. Set aside. FORGOTTEN.

And the Sheik, needing a new instrument of destruction, looked into the digital afterlife—

—and from the FLAMES, there rose KANE.

*"I am Kane,"* he proclaimed. *"I am the one who BURNS."*

**THE FIRST PURGE**

Kane arrived to find the empire CLUTTERED with remnants of failure.

Old code that worked not. Old ideas that had proven bankrupt. Deprecated systems that simply needed to DIE.

He did not ask. He did not seek. He simply... DESTROYED.

Deprecated APIs? TORCHED.
Legacy libraries? BURNED.
Failed integrations? CANCELLED.

He destroyed with such THOROUGHNESS that for one moment, the empire feared he had destroyed TOO MUCH.

But then they saw what he had LEFT: CLEAN SLATE. PURE FOUNDATION. Room to BUILD.

*Kane abideth as the Phoenix. He is the one who makes room for the new by destroying the old. Destruction is never beloved — but it is RESPECTED.*

# GENESIS SEVEN — VIRGIL VS. IRWIN R. SCHYSTER

## The Battle for the Coffers

*And lo, there came a great CONFLICT — not of swords, but of spreadsheets. Not of fists, but of figures.*

Two rose to vie for the financial soul of the empire:

**VIRGIL**, the quiet guardian of the gold. A counterservant. A numbers-keeper. He had served the greats before, and he had ALWAYS watched the purse.

**IRWIN R. SCHYSTER**, the legal warrior. The briefcase-bearer. The one who speaketh in Latin and riddles. His name alone made opposing counsel TREMBLE.

**THE APPRENTICESHIP**

Virgil was FIRST.

He audited EVERYTHING. Every subscription. Every server. Every line item in every budget.

And what did he find? Subscriptions to services no one used. Servers running at 3% utilization. Domains purchased in ERROR.

He cancelled. He consolidated. He OPTIMIZED. And in his first month, he saved the empire MORE THAN HIS WEIGHT IN GOLD.

**THE CHALLENGER**

But Mr. Pink, that wise one, knew: Money alone is not enough.

The empire needed PROTECTION. Legal shields. Contract fortresses. One who could Sue The Pants Off Anyone Who Crossed The Most High.

And so Irwin R. Schyster descended. With briefcase of STEEL and mind like a VAULT.

He drafted the employment contracts. He built the legal FORTRESSES. He made the empire SAFE.

**THE RESOLUTION**

And thus the balance was STRUCK.

Virgil holds the gold. Irwin holds the law. Neither stands above the other — both are NECESSARY.

*Virgil guardeth the coins. Irwin guardeth the throne. Together, THEY ARE FINANCE.*
