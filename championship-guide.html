Welcome to the FIRST Championship

The 2026 FIRST® Championship presented by BAE Systems will bring together thousands of students from around the world for the ultimate celebration of STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math) and innovation.

This season — REBUILT℠ — every artifact we uncover holds a story. Each tool, each innovation, and each work of art connects us to the people and ideas that came before us. Using STEM skills and teamwork, today we can dig deeper into discoveries than ever before.

Get ready to have fun and be part of something extraordinary.

Health & Safety

⚠ Important: All children under 12 must always be accompanied by an adult in the Pit area. Face coverings and vaccinations are not required but are recommended. If you are feeling ill, please do not attend the event.
🚗 Pit Area Rules
Sandals, open-toed shoes, and Crocs are not allowed in the Pits, fields, or teamwork areas. Wear proper footwear at all times.
🚽 Dress Code
Wear relaxed, fun, and appropriate clothing. You represent your team and FIRST. Body paint in lieu of clothes is not appropriate.
🔧 Safety Glasses
Required in the Pits and on the competition fields. Glasses are provided at entry — bring your own or use ours.
🚑 Medical
Medical personnel on site. In case of emergency, locate the nearest event staff member or go directly to a medical station.
💭 Mental Health
Supporting mental health is a priority. Resources available on site — ask at Guest Services.
🚨 Youth Protection
Report any youth protection or safety concerns immediately to a staff member or designated reporter.

Security & Prohibited Items

🚡 Enhanced Security: Metal detection screenings at ALL event entrances. Expanded perimeter includes Discovery Green and Avenidas de las Americas. Plan for additional entry time.

Prohibited items include but are not limited to:

  • Weapons of any kind
  • Explosives or flammable materials
  • Glass containers
  • Large bags or backpacks (check size limits at entry)
  • Outside food or beverages (sealed water bottles allowed)
  • Pets (service animals excepted)
  • Laser pointers
  • Professional camera equipment (tripods, large lenses) without press credentials

Canine dog teams will be present at loading docks during team load-in for vehicle screening. Thank you for your cooperation.
